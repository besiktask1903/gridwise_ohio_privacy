Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

GridWise Ohio ("we", "our", or "the app") is a free utility rate comparison tool for Ohio residents. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

GridWise Ohio collects minimal data to function:

  • Utility selections — Your chosen gas and electric utility are stored locally on your device to personalize the app experience. This data never leaves your device.
  • No account required — We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personal identifiers.
  • No location data — We do not access or store your location.

Data Storage

All preferences (your selected utilities) are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. We do not operate servers that store user data. Rate data is fetched from publicly available sources and is not personalized.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Apple StoreKit for optional tip jar purchases. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Analytics

GridWise Ohio does not use any analytics or tracking SDKs. We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or device information.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is a general-purpose utility comparison tool suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at iamdebugging@gmail.com.
